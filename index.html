Palm Beach Lake Trail

The trail is on the west side of Palm Beach, along the Lake Worth Lagoon.

Sunset Avenue to North Lake Way

The trail runs about 5 miles from Peruvian Avenue (South Lake Drive) at the south, to North Lake Way just past the Sailfish Club at the north.

South Lake Trail

he South Lake Trail in Palm Beach is a bike and walking path that follows the intercoastal waterway.

Pinehurst Trail

Pinehurst Trail is a 3.4 mile loop trail located near West Palm Beach, Florida that features a lake and is rated as moderate.
